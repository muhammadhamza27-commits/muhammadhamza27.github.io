I have always been a builder. In childhood, it was Lego blocks; today, it is code.

I was in the eighth grade when I first discovered the world of websites, HTML, and core programming. That early realization anchored my path, showing me how simple structural text can generate living, interactive spaces.

Back in my childhood, I used to spend hours painting and drawing. I still return to canvas and brush sometimes—you have to keep your inner child alive as you grow up. But mostly, that creative energy is now poured directly into designing elegant user interfaces and writing sophisticated algorithms in Python.

Beyond these technical curiosities, my primary academic focus remains steady as I prepare for a disciplined professional track in Chartered Accountancy right here in Karachi.
